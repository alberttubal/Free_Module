Features Overview

📁

Upload & Share Academic Files

Organize lecture notes, past exams, and study guides for your course and semester.

🗣️

Experience Wall

Honest reflections from upperclassmen about professors, labs, and tips that matter.

🔍

Smart Search

Find documents and posts by course, professor, or semester with filters and tags.

🌟

Ratings & Feedback

Rate resources and leave feedback so the best materials surface for everyone.

🎤

Ask an Upperclassman

Post questions and get direct answers from students with real experience.

🧭

Freshman Survival Guide

Curated checklists and tips to help new students navigate campus life and academics.
